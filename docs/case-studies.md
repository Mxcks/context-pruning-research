# Case Studies

## Status

No production case studies are currently verified in this repository.

Earlier draft material described example outcomes across software development, research synthesis, support automation, and creative production. Those examples should be treated as illustrative scenarios, not measured real-world results.

## Illustrative Scenarios

The following scenarios describe where context pruning may be useful:

- software development across multiple services or repositories
- research synthesis across multiple domains
- support workflows where product-specific context must stay isolated
- creative workflows where brand or style context must not bleed across projects

## What A Valid Case Study Needs

A future case study should include:

- a public or synthetic dataset
- baseline behavior
- pruning configuration
- exact commands or evaluation workflow
- raw measurements
- limitations
- whether the result measures storage/context size, model quality, human productivity, or another outcome

## Current Guidance

Use this page as a placeholder for future evidence-backed studies. Do not cite it as proof of quantified performance or business impact yet.
